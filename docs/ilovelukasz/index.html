A tribute to my good friend Łukasz Kubicki. 

Lukasz being extremely intelligent I see his mind as his biggest strength and also his biggest enemy.


I tried to convey this idea with a triangle puzzle that never gets to be solved.  
Other triangles (squares and circles) keep dancing around, not only adding to the composition but also just add noise.


INSTRUCTIONS:

the reflective button reacts to a click of a mouse.

the thinking button reacts to a hover of the mouse or a drag on a mobile.

the overthinking is automatic, you have no control over it.
